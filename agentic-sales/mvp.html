Foundation first

Strategy → ICP → Personas → Messages

Before sending a single email, RevenueFlow establishes your strategic foundation. Every message is then generated from this full context — not generic templates.

1

Strategy

Market definition, value proposition, competitive differentiation. One active strategy per organization.

2

Ideal Customer Profiles (ICPs)

WHO to target. Firmographics, behavioral signals, fit/disqualification criteria. Multiple ICPs per strategy.

3

Buyer Personas with JTBD

WHY they buy. Jobs-To-Be-Done, pain points, objections, trigger events. Multiple personas per ICP.

4

Projects

Each project targets ONE ICP + ONE Persona. This focus makes outreach precise, not scattered.

AI-Generated Messages
